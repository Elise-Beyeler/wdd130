River Tides White Water Rafting

Our purpose is to bring a happy, exhilerating experience to all who wish to go on a white water rafting trip with us! Our mission is to ensure safety while having fun and being informative.

History

Our company was founded on January 21st, 2000. We have continuosly striven to put safety of our riders first and foremost, with fun at the second!

Adventure Awaits You!

You can choose to ride the river with a team of at least four people, with one or two guides at your side(above), or you can choose to go on your own in a kayak(below).

During the day, the rafting tour will make stops for lunch and rest breaks, and have frequent places of calm water to swim in.

All your equipment will be provided for you. Helmets are required for your saffety.

Have fun while you're here! It's an exhilerating ride!
